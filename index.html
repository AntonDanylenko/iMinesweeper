The purpose of the game is to flag all the cells on the board which you know have mines. You lose if you set off a mine cell. Every non-mine cell you open will tell you the total number of mines in the eight neighboring cells. Once you are sure that a cell contains a mine, press the flag button or hit SPACE to switch to the flag and flag it. Once you have flagged all the mines around an open cell, you can quickly open the remaining non-mine cells by long-pressing on the cell or holding SHIFT and clicking.
